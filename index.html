Do you provide preventive health checkups?

Yes, preventive health checkups are available with prior booking. We encourage routine screenings to detect potential health concerns early and support long-term wellbeing.

Do you offer diagnostic and laboratory services?

Yes, comprehensive diagnostic and laboratory services are available on-site with scheduled appointments. We ensure accurate testing, timely reports, and coordinated care for effective treatment planning.

Do you offer walk-in or urgent care appointments?

Yes, walk-in and urgent care services are available based on doctor availability. We prioritize emergency and critical cases to ensure timely medical attention.

Can I consult a doctor without a prior appointment?

Yes, consultations without prior appointments may be possible depending on availability. However, we recommend scheduling in advance to reduce waiting time and ensure convenience.

How often should I have a general health check-up?

An annual health check-up is recommended, or as advised by your doctor based on your age and medical history.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Please bring a valid ID, previous medical records, current medication list, and insurance details (if applicable).

Do you offer preventive health screenings?

Yes, we provide comprehensive preventive screenings tailored to age, gender, and risk factors.

How can I maintain better overall health?

Maintain a balanced diet, exercise regularly, get adequate sleep, and attend routine medical check-ups.

When should I seek immediate medical care?

Seek urgent care if you experience severe pain, chest discomfort, breathing difficulty, or sudden health changes.

How can I book an appointment?

Appointments can be scheduled online, by phone, or directly at the hospital reception.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel by contacting us at least 24 hours in advance.

Do you accept walk-in patients?

Walk-ins are accepted based on doctor availability, but scheduled appointments are recommended.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

Please arrive 15–20 minutes early to complete any required paperwork.

Will I receive appointment confirmation?

Yes, you will receive confirmation via call, SMS, or email after booking.

What medical specialties do you offer?

We provide a wide range of specialties including general medicine, cardiology, pediatrics, orthopedics, and more

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, our emergency department operates 24/7 for urgent medical needs.

Are diagnostic tests available on-site?

Yes, we offer laboratory services, imaging, and other diagnostic facilities within the hospital.

Do you provide surgical services?

Yes, we perform both minor and major surgical procedures with advanced equipment.

Are specialist consultations available?

Yes, experienced specialists are available by appointment or referral.

How are patient records maintained?

All records are stored securely using advanced digital systems with strict privacy controls.

Can I request a copy of my medical records?

Yes, patients can request copies by submitting a formal application with valid identification.

Is my medical information confidential?

Absolutely. We follow strict confidentiality policies and healthcare privacy regulations.

How long are medical records stored?

Records are retained according to healthcare regulatory requirements and hospital policy.

Can I update my personal information?
